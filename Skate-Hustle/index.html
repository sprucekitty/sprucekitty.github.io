The story

The Modern Skate Family.

At the intersection of Skate Culture and Art.

We are a skating family building a brand together. Dad codes and creates art and kid earns "sweat equity" by handling logistics, packing, and learning real business realities.

Our operating principles are strictly sustainable, ethical, and quality-driven. If the canvas fails, the art is worthless.

Designed in and printed in Helsinki.
